NIT Manipur was established in 2010 as an Institute of National Importance. It aims to provide high-quality technical education and foster a strong research culture. The campus is located in a scenic and peaceful environment at Langol, Imphal.

NIT Manipur offers B.Tech, M.Tech, M.Sc., and Ph.D. programs across various disciplines including CSE, ECE, EEE, Civil, and Mechanical Engineering. The curriculum is designed to meet global standards and is regularly updated.

The campus of NIT Manipur offers modern facilities including smart classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a central library, hostels, sports complexes, and high-speed internet. The institute encourages a healthy balance of academics and extracurriculars.
